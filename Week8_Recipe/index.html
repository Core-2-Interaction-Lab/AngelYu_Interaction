Mango Lassi Receipe

Mango Lassi is an indian cuisine, best for the summer when you are in need of a nice, refreshing, fruity drink. Please be mindful that this drink has dairy!

Ingredients

  • 2 canned mangos
  • 2 cups of greek or mango yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 cups of ice
  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Prepare a blender
  2. Put in 2 canned mango sin the blender
  3. Put the rest of the ingredients inside
  4. Blend for 5 minutes
  5. Serve!
